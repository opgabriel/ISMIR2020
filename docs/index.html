Detecting Collaboration Profiles in Success-based Music Genre Networks

ISMIR 2020 Pre-publication Release

As the landscape of the music industry becomes more complex, the strategy of reaching a wider audience through collaborative means has proven its effectiveness in maintaining the presence of artists in the market. To better understand which are the real factors that compose a successful collaboration, in this work, we analyze and identify collaboration profiles in success-based music genre networks.
